标题

标题要求：简明扼要，必要时附上个人信息，如student ID

常见title：

PhD application 博士申请

Application for Scholarship 奖学金申请

Questions for essay 作业提问

Excused Absence 请假

Asking for an appointment 约见时间

Extension for XXX 要求延期

称呼

Dear Professor + Last Name

Dear Dr. + Last Name

正文

问候语

Hope all is well.

I hope this email finds you well.

We send you our best wishes.

Warm greetings

Good Morning/Afternoon.

Happy Holidays.

I hope your week is going smoothly.

自我介绍

进行自我介绍，点名姓名、班级、Student ID、与教授认识的方式。

My name is XXX and I am your student in XXX class.

邮件目的

简单明了的使用一句话告诉教授邮件的目的或者重点。

I am writing to inquire XXX

I am writing to reference to XXXX

I am writing to express my XXX

详细内容

要求清晰 clear、正确 correct、简明 concise

提出疑问：I have a question about the essay due next Tuesday.

推荐信：I am hoping you would be willing to write a strong/positive letter of recommendation.

请求：Could you please tell me what to do next? I would be grateful if you could give me more time to …

约meet：Could I please book to see you during Office Hour on Friday? At 2 pm?

致谢落款

表示感谢：

Thank you for your time. Have a great rest of your day.

落款：

Sincerely, 最正式的

Best regards,

Best wishes,

Best,

Cheers,

常用语句

回复邮件

  • Thank you for your email.

  • Thank you for the quick response.

  • I appreciate the update.

  • I apologize for the delay. (formal)

  • Sorry it took me a while to get back to you. (informal)

follow up email

  • I wanted to follow up with you regrading …

  • As we discuessed in our last meeting.

  • As promised …

  • I wanted to get back to you about …

  • Thank you for your time.

表达请求

  • Could you please help me with …

  • May I kindly ask …

  • I would really appreciate your help …

  • If it is not too much trouble, could you help …

  • I am struggling with … Could you lend a

  • It would be helpful if you could …

  • I was hoping you could assist me with

  • I would appreciate your guidance on …

  • Would you be able to show me …

  • Could I ask for your expertise on …

约meet

  • Would it be possible to schedule a meeting with you so that we can discuss further this?

  • Would either [two choices of date and time] be a good time for you?

  • Would either [two choices of date and time] work for you?

  • I am [Your Name], and I would love the opportunity to speak with you about [Topic]. Would you be available for a brief chat sometime in the next few days?

  • I am writing because I am interested in learning more about your work as a [job title]. Might you have time for a brief discussion so that I can ask some questions and learn more?
